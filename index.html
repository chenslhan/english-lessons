About the Lessons

Hi — I’m Han. I focus on helping adult learners begin speaking Mandarin with confidence using practical, everyday topics and lots of speaking practice.

Curriculum Roadmap: Beginner to Intermediate

I will guide you from your very first word of Mandarin to becoming a confident, fluent conversationalist.

Level What You Will Achieve Exam Target Est. Lessons
Beginner Low to High Beginner Master Pinyin, Tones, and essential grammar for **Survival Chinese**. You will be able to handle travel, simple requests, greetings, and basic daily transactions. HSK 1 - 2 15 - 30
Intermediate Low to High Intermediate Achieve fluent, spontaneous conversation. You will be able to discuss work, lifestyle, express opinions, and handle more complex social and **Workplace** situations. HSK 3 - 4 30 - 60+

* Lesson counts are estimates and vary based on individual learning pace and homework completion.
