How I Teach

My lessons are designed to be effective and structured.

  • Universal Curriculum: We follow a set syllabus so you never feel lost.
  • Placement Test: Before starting, I provide a universal placement test to ensure you are learning at the exact right level.
  • Practice Makes Perfect: I provide homework and worksheets for you to practice online between sessions.
